How does it work?

1. Take the quiz!
2. Answer honestly
3. Listen to the suggestions!

Information

Target audience:
People interested in lowering their carbon footprint.

Goal:
To help stop carbon pollution by making people more aware of their effects on the enviroment.

Description:
CarbonBuddy is an application which gives you daily surveys about your carbon footprint. The app builds a profile based on your answers and gives you daily recommendations and tips to help lower your carbon footprint.
